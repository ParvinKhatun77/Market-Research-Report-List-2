<p><strong>The Advanced Surgical Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image intensifier C-arms</li><li>Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Surgical Imaging System market comprises primarily two types: Image Intensifier C-arms and Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms). Image Intensifier C-arms utilize an image intensifier tube to enhance image quality during surgical procedures, providing real-time imaging with effective radiation doses. In contrast, FPD C-arms use digital flat panel detectors, offering superior image resolution, reduced radiation exposure, and enhanced workflow efficiency. Both types support minimally invasive surgeries, facilitating precise diagnostics and treatment in various medical settings.</p></p>
