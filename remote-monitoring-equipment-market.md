<p><p>The remote monitoring equipment market is divided into two main types: external and implantable. External monitoring devices are used outside the body and include wearables like smartwatches, fitness trackers, and portable sensors that track various health metrics. Implantable devices, on the other hand, are surgically placed inside the body to monitor critical conditions and deliver therapies, such as pacemakers and glucose monitors. Both types enhance patient care by providing real-time data to healthcare providers and improving health management.</p></p>
