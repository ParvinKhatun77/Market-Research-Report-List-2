<p><p>The acrylic fiber market is primarily divided into two types: staple fiber and filament fiber. Staple fiber consists of short strands, typically used in applications like textiles and carpets, offering a soft texture and warmth. Filament fiber, on the other hand, consists of continuous strands, ideal for applications requiring durability and strength, such as outdoor fabrics and upholstery. Each type serves distinct purposes in various industries, driven by their unique characteristics and performance attributes. Both types are crucial in the fashion and home goods sectors.</p></p>
