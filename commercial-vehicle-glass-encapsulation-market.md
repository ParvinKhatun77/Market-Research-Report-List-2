<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Glass Encapsulation Market encompasses various materials used for bonding and sealing glass components in vehicles. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) combines rubber-like properties with thermoplastic characteristics, ensuring resilience and ease of processing.
