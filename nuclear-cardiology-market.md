<p><p>Nuclear cardiology is a specialized branch of cardiology that utilizes radioactive materials and imaging techniques to assess heart function and diagnose cardiovascular diseases. The aging population and lifestyle changes contributing to heart-related ailments have further propelled market expansion. </p><p>Emerging trends include the integration of artificial intelligence with nuclear imaging, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and efficiency. Additionally, advancements in radiopharmaceuticals and the development of hybrid imaging systems are expected to improve patient outcomes and broaden the application of nuclear cardiology. </p><p>The Nuclear Cardiology Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period, reflecting the growing reliance on advanced imaging techniques in cardiovascular diagnostics and the potential for increased adoption in clinical practices worldwide.</p></p>
