<p><p>The fiber cement board market comprises three main types based on density: high, medium, and low. High-density fiber cement boards are robust and suitable for structural applications, offering excellent strength and durability. Medium-density boards strike a balance between weight and sturdiness, making them ideal for both interior and exterior uses. Low-density fiber cement boards are lightweight, often employed in non-structural applications, such as interior wall cladding and decorative elements. Each type serves distinct needs across construction and design sectors.</p></p>
