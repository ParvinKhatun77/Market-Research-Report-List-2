<p><p>The electrostatic separator market is divided into two main types: dry and wet electrostatic separators. Dry separators primarily rely on electrostatic forces to separate materials without the use of water, making them suitable for processing fine particles and dry materials. Wet separators, on the other hand, utilize a liquid medium to enhance separation efficiency, particularly for materials that are difficult to process in dry form. Both types are essential in applications like mineral processing, recycling, and waste management.</p></p>
