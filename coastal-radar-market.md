<p><p>The coastal radar market is primarily categorized into three types: X-Band Radar, S-Band Radar, and Other radars. X-Band Radar operates at higher frequencies, offering high-resolution imagery for short-range applications, ideal for monitoring small vessels and coastal activities. S-Band Radar, with a lower frequency, provides longer-range detection, making it suitable for tracking large ships and weather patterns. Other market types encompass specialized radars, including L-Band and C-Band systems, serving niche applications in maritime surveillance and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
