<p><p>The multilayer cast film line market is segmented primarily into CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film lines and CPE (Cast Polyethylene) film lines. CPP film lines are used to produce lightweight, versatile films known for their excellent clarity and moisture resistance, making them ideal for packaging applications. In contrast, CPE film lines focus on polyethylene films, which offer superior flexibility and durability, often utilized in various industrial and consumer packaging solutions. Both types cater to diverse industries requiring tailored film properties.</p></p>
