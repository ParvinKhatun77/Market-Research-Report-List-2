<p><p>Smart Ring Main Units (RMUs) are compact electrical distribution devices used in medium voltage networks to enhance reliability and automation. They come in three main types: Solid Insulated RMUs utilize solid dielectric materials for insulation, offering a compact design and reduced environmental impact. Gas Insulated RMUs employ sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation, allowing for smaller installations in urban areas. Air Insulated RMUs use air as an insulating medium, making them cost-effective for larger outdoor applications, each serving specific operational needs and environments.</p></p>
