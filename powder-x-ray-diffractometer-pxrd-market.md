<p><p>A Powder X-ray Diffractometer (PXRD) is an analytical tool used to determine the crystallographic structure of materials, primarily in powder form. It functions by directing X-ray beams at the sample and measuring the angle and intensity of the diffracted rays, allowing researchers to identify the phase composition, crystal size, and other structural information.</p><p>The PXRD market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing demand in sectors such as pharmaceuticals, materials science, and nanotechnology. The rise in research activities and technological advancements contribute significantly to market momentum.
