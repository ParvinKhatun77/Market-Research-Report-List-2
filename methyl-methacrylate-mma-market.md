<p><p>Methyl Methacrylate (MMA) is a versatile acrylic monomer used in various applications, including adhesives, coatings, and plastics. The market for MMA can be segmented by production methods: the ACH method involves the condensation of acetone and hydrogen cyanide, while the Isobutylene method utilizes isobutylene and methacrylic acid as feedstocks.
