<p><strong>The Redundancy Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DPST</li><li>DPDT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Redundancy switches are crucial in ensuring reliable power and signal management in various applications. The Double Pole Single Throw (DPST) switch allows for dual circuits to be controlled simultaneously on or off, providing basic redundancy. In contrast, the Double Pole Double Throw (DPDT) switch enables two separate circuits to be directed towards one of two outputs, allowing for more complex configurations. Both types are used in critical systems where uninterrupted operation is essential, enhancing reliability and failover capabilities.</p></p>
