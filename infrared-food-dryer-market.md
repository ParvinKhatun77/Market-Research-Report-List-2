<p><ul><li>Continuous Type</li><li>Discontinuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared food dryer market can be categorized into two main types: continuous and discontinuous. Continuous type dryers facilitate a streamlined process where food products move through a drying tunnel in a constant flow, allowing for high efficiency and large-scale production. In contrast, discontinuous type dryers operate in batches, making them suitable for smaller operations or varied product types. Each type caters to different production needs, with continuous systems favoring high output, while discontinuous systems offer flexibility and control over drying times.</p></p>
