Bulk biomass briquettes are large compressed blocks of organic material, offering a convenient option for heating and energy generation, typically used in industrial applications. Biomass pellets, on the other hand, are small, uniform cylindrical forms made from finely processed wood particles, ideal for residential heating and small-scale applications due to their high density and energy efficiency. Both types promote renewable energy utilization and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
