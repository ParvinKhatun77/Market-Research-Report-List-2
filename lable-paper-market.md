<p><p>The label paper market comprises various types, primarily wet-glue labels and self-adhesive labels. Wet-glue labels are applied using adhesive liquids, commonly found in beverage and food packaging, offering durability and tamper resistance. In contrast, self-adhesive labels feature a pre-applied adhesive backing, allowing for easy application without additional glue. These are widely used in retail, logistics, and product labeling due to their convenience and versatility. Both types cater to distinct industries and consumer needs, driving market growth.</p></p>
