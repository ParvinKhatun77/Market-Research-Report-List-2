<p><p>Metal engraving machines are categorized into two primary types: manual and automatic. Manual engraving machines require human operation, allowing for intricate designs and personalized touches, often preferred by artisans and small workshops. In contrast, automatic engraving machines utilize computer numerical control (CNC) technology, enabling high-speed and high-precision engraving suitable for mass production and consistent output. The choice between manual and automatic types depends on factors like design complexity, production volume, and desired level of craftsmanship.</p></p>
