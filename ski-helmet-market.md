<p><ul><li>ABS Material</li><li>PC Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ski helmet market can be categorized primarily into two types based on material: ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) and PC (Polycarbonate). ABS helmets are known for their durability and impact resistance, making them popular among recreational skiers. In contrast, PC helmets are lighter and offer superior strength, often preferred for professional and high-performance skiing due to their ability to withstand higher impacts. Both materials aim to enhance safety and comfort while catering to different skier preferences and needs.</p></p>
