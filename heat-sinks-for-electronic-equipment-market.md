<p><strong>The Heat Sinks for Electronic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Heat Sinks</li><li>Passive Heat Sinks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat sinks for electronic equipment are crucial for maintaining optimal operating temperatures. Active heat sinks incorporate fans or pumps to enhance heat dissipation, making them effective in high-performance applications where efficient cooling is essential. In contrast, passive heat sinks rely on natural convection and conduction, utilizing materials with high thermal conductivity without mechanical components. While active systems offer superior cooling capabilities, passive heat sinks are often quieter, more reliable, and require less maintenance, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
