<p><p>The cigar humidor market is primarily divided into two types: electric and non-electric humidors. Electric humidors are equipped with built-in temperature and humidity controls, offering precise conditions for cigar storage, often appealing to serious collectors. Non-electric humidors rely on passive methods, such as hygrometers and water sources, to maintain humidity levels, making them more affordable and portable. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with electric options focusing on convenience and advanced features, while non-electric models emphasize simplicity and classic charm.</p></p>
