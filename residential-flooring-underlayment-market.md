<p><p>The residential flooring underlayment market consists of various types, each serving specific purposes. Cement Backer Units (CBU) provide a moisture-resistant base for tile installations, while polyethylene acts as a moisture barrier, protecting flooring from humidity. Rubber underlayment offers soundproofing and shock absorption, making it ideal for multi-story buildings. Cork is eco-friendly, providing thermal insulation and sound control. Plywood serves as a sturdy foundation, enhancing durability. Other materials may include foam and mineral fiber options, expanding versatility for different flooring needs.</p></p>
