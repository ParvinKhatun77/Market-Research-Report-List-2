<p><p>Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) can be classified into two main market types: Embedded Physical Cyber Systems (EP-CPS) and Information Technology Cyber Physical Systems (IT-CPS). EP-CPS focus on integrating embedded systems with physical processes, primarily seen in industries like manufacturing and automotive, enhancing automation and real-time control. In contrast, IT-CPS emphasize the convergence of information technology with physical environments, found in smart buildings and healthcare, leveraging data analytics and connectivity to optimize operations and improve decision-making.</p></p>
