<p><p>Specialty surfactants are specialized chemical agents used to lower surface tension in various applications, including personal care, textiles, and agriculture. They are categorized into four main types: anionic surfactants, which carry a negative charge and are effective in cleaning and foaming; cationic surfactants, with a positive charge, commonly used for conditioning and antimicrobial properties; amphoteric surfactants, which can carry both positive and negative charges, making them versatile in formulations; and other categories, including nonionic surfactants, which are neutral and used for various industrial purposes.</p></p>
