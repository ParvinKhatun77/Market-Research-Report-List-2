<p><p>The aerospace semi-finished titanium materials market comprises four primary types: rod, sheet, plate, and tube. Rods are cylindrical and used for machined parts and fasteners, offering high strength-to-weight ratios. Sheets are flat products ideal for aircraft skins and structural components. Plates are thicker than sheets, employed in structural applications needing durability and support. Tubes provide lightweight, strong solutions for hydraulic systems and structural frames, making them essential for aerospace design and performance. Each type plays a critical role in aircraft manufacturing and maintenance.</p></p>
